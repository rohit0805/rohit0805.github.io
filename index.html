ImageHolder Scroll Effect

As we scroll down by 200px,400px and 600px the two placeholder images will scroll from the left and right side of the screen respectively. The transition time is 1s and the transition-timing-function for these images is cubic-bezier. Now, as we scroll up again the image will again go to their initial position. And after scrolling 200px the Random Text start to fade.
